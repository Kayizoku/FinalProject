The Third Problem

The third problem is that you will have very little control on what shows up on the web and what you do on the web.

You will have people watching your every movement. That means you will have no privacy what so ever, and no option to be anonymous.
The scary thing here is that your ISP might be able to sell your private information like browsing histories and other things.

Your ISP can also manipulate what will show up on different websites and they will monitor every thing you do. They have full control over how YOU use the internet. This means that the cable companies will no longer be obligated to deliver full internet access to everyone and every website.
