Third Problem

The third problem is that you will have very little control over what shows up on the web and what you do on the web.

You will have people watching your every move. That means, you will have no privacy what so ever, and no option to be anonymous.
The scary thing here is that your ISP might be able to sell your private information like browsing history and other things.

Your ISP can also manipulate what will show up on different websites, which will make it possible for them to censor controversial content. They will have full control over how YOU use the internet. This means that the cable companies will no longer be obligated to deliver full internet access to everyone and every website.
