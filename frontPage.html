Net Neutrality

And why you should care!

Net neutraility is basically internets guiding principle. It preserves our right to communicate freely online. The internet currently gives you the same download speed on every website you visit, you pay a set amount of money every month to get internet access and you don't have anyone monitoring every click you make on the world wide web (presumably). And now, someone wants to take that freedom away from us.
