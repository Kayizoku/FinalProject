What is the purpose of this website?

We want to promote the importance of net neutrality.
Most people don't know how fragile the web security is
and live their lives, using social media,
thinking they can do whatever without it ever changing.
But it could, very easily, and we want to prevent it
by making people aware of what is really going on.
