The First Problem

Recently, a big company in the US called the FCC, announced that they will basically just destroy the net neutrality laws.
Why is this a problem you ask? This is problem because your internet service providers will be able to slow down websites of their choice.
Let me give you an example.

You are paying for your internet through the internet, one day a month. There's one day left until you have to pay, and you go to their website to pay. When you try to log in, it takes AGES to have the site load up. You have to go through multiple steps, which takes hours, and then, you miss your opportunity to pay. You get extra fees and so on.

This is not ok

So you decide to change your ISP, so you'll go look around on the internet. You find another website to a new ISP and try to open the website. "Oh whoops, this site has been blocked". What do you do?
