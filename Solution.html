Solution

To fight the FCC and protect net neutrality is not easy.

We can win if we stand together, and only then, we can overthorw the FCC and make their bill not pass through. But keep in mind, we don't have a lot of time left. In a matter of fact, there's only 3 weeks left.

Your voice counts and it will be heard! So please, do your best to save net neutrality. The future of the internet as we know it is at stake.

The First Solution

The first and maybe the easiest thing you can do, is to submit a ticket to the FCC saying you want to restore internet freedom. They will read your ticket and take it to concideration with all the other tickets.

You can do this here

The crucial thing here is not to harass someone in any way, that might lead to more negative outcomes than positive ones. Just state the facts and personal thoughts on why an open internet is important to you.
